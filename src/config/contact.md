# Description

C4DM Studios is a high-end university recording studio and research facility operating from within the School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at Queen Mary University of London.
Located on the ground floor of the Electronic Engineering building, C4DM Studios is composed of three spaces: the Performance Lab (G2B), the Control Room (G2C), and the Listening/Live Room (G4).
C4DM Studios primarily serves to support research and teaching activities relating to the fields of audio engineering, music production, digital signal processing, human-computer interaction, music informatics, music perception and cognition, augmented instruments, immersive technologies, AI & music, and performance studies.
Established by the Centre for Digital Music (C4DM) and Cognitive Science (CogSci) research groups, C4DM Studios are currently a core facility for the Centres for Doctoral Training in Media & Arts Technology (MAT) and AI & Music (AIM).
